Game in Unity3D

Play as a Space Commander 900 years in the future. Take control of your space ship to destroy alien ships that threaten the galaxy. Fight to protect Earth as you destroy asteroids and enemies that stand in your way. Kill the mothership that commands the army and win.

My final project for a game scripting class. It takes the classic game 1942 1000 years in the future. To make the game, I used art assets and ships from the Unity Asset Store.
