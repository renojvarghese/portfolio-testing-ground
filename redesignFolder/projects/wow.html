World of Warcraft Matching App

A friend finding and dating app for your avatar

A fun app to play with InVision. I based the UI around Tinder while using parchment and wood textures that could be found in World of WarCraft. If the app were created, it would could connect allow people to connect their characters to find avatars on their server who match their interests. A player could download the app on a phone and set up accounts for each of his/her characters using a Battle.net account. The app would also use the messaging/mail system found in the game for communication.
