Antibiotics Application

Collaboration w/ Connecticut Children's Medical Center

The idea for the application came from two doctors who work at the Connecticut Children's Medical Center (CCMC). Working in different countries, they noticed how different clinics would have varying antibiotics. They wanted an app that could provide information on how to combat diseases based on the what medicine was available in that specific clinic

The solution was to create an app that would deliver information on different antibiotics, the bacteria that cause these diseases, and symptoms to identify a condition. The data for the medical information would be provided by the doctors. The following app is the infrastructure in which the information would be presented to a doctor and the way doctors at CCMC would submit the information
